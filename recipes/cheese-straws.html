Quick cheese straws

Description

Delicious cheesy sticks, perfect for dipping, just as good on their own - ready in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 350g pack ready-rolled puff pastry
  • four handfuls grated parmesan (or vegetarian alternative)
  • handful flour, to roll pastry on

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 220c/fan 200c/gas 7
  2. Unroll a puff pastry, scatter over a couple of handfuls of grated Parmesan, then fold in half
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out to the thickness of a Â£1 coin. Cut into 1cm strips, then twist the strips 3-4 times
  4. Lay on a baking sheet, scatter over more cheese and bake for 12 mins, or until golden
  5. Leave to cool, then keep in an airtight container for up to 2 days. As these cheese straws are very delicate, roll them in kitchen paper before packing into a plastic container
